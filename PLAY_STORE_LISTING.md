## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Budgetly automatically tracks your expenses by reading bank transaction SMS — no manual entry needed. All your financial data stays 100% on your device. No cloud sync, no servers, no ads.

**🤖 AUTOMATIC EXPENSE TRACKING**
Budgetly reads bank SMS messages and instantly categorises your spending. Supports HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Axis, Kotak, and all major UPI apps including PhonePe, Google Pay, and Paytm. No login required.

**📊 SMART CATEGORISATION**
Expenses are automatically sorted into 17 categories: Food & Dining, Groceries, Shopping, Entertainment, Transport, Health, Bills, Fuel, Education, Investments, Insurance, EMI, Subscriptions, and more.

Changed a category? Update it once and every transaction from that merchant updates automatically.

**💵 CASH & MANUAL EXPENSES**
Add cash expenses manually alongside your card and UPI transactions for a complete financial picture.

**📅 BILL REMINDERS**
Never miss a payment. Set recurring bill reminders for rent, EMIs, utilities, and subscriptions. Get notified 1–7 days before each due date.

**📱 SUBSCRIPTION TRACKER**
See all your subscriptions in one place — Netflix, Spotify, Jio, Airtel, Amazon Prime, and more. Instantly know your monthly and yearly subscription spend.

**🎯 SMART BUDGETS**
Set monthly budgets for each spending category. Get alerts when you reach 90% of your budget so you can course-correct before overspending.

**📈 VISUAL INSIGHTS**
Understand your money with beautiful charts:
• Donut chart — spending by category this month
• Bar chart — budget vs actual spending comparison  
• Line chart — income and expense trends over 6 months

**🔒 100% PRIVATE & SECURE**
• All data stored on your device — never uploaded
• No cloud sync, no Google Drive backup
• Optional biometric (fingerprint/face) app lock
• Export your data as CSV anytime
• Works completely offline

**More features:**
✓ Swipe to delete expenses
✓ Full-text search across all transactions
✓ SMS auto-import on first launch (last 90 days)
✓ Re-scan SMS anytime from Settings
✓ Filter by category, date range, payment mode
✓ Dark mode support
✓ UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card, NEFT, RTGS auto-detected
